# Legal Content Policy

This project uses a fixed legal content hierarchy.

## Binding Version

The German legal pages are the legally binding original version:

- `/impressum`
- `/datenschutz`
- `/agb`

## English Convenience Version

English legal pages are complete international convenience versions. Every English legal page must show this notice:

> This English version is provided for convenience. The German version is the legally binding version. In case of discrepancies, the German version prevails.

## Other Locales

French, Spanish, Simplified Chinese, and Arabic must not contain automatically translated or invented legal texts.

These locales should show:

- `Non-binding placeholder`
- `Official legal version available in English.`
- A link to the English legal version.

Do not add legal translations for `fr`, `es`, `zh`, or `ar` unless they are professionally prepared and explicitly approved.
